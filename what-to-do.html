what to do

Food & Wine

Italy is famous for its delicious cuisine, including pasta, pizza, and gelato. Don't miss the chance to enjoy local wines, especially in regions like Tuscany and Piedmont.

Art & Culture

Italy is home to some of the world's most renowned art and architecture. Visit the Uffizi Gallery in Florence, the Vatican Museums in Rome, and the canals of Venice.

Nature

Explore Italy's stunning landscapes, from the rolling hills of Tuscany to the breathtaking Amalfi Coast. Don't forget to visit national parks like Gran Paradiso and Cinque Terre.

Shopping & Markets

Italy offers a variety of shopping experiences, from high-end fashion in Milan to local markets in Florence. Be sure to explore the vibrant street markets for unique finds.

Sports

Sports are an essential part of Italian culture. Enjoy watching football (soccer) matches, cycling through the countryside, or even participating in local sports events.
